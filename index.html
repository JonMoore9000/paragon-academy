What is Paragon Academy?

Paragon Academy is striving to be a place for new players to Paragon to learn the game and gain experience in a fun, toxic free environment. We are here to help guide players along as they work towards bettering their skills and learning what it means to be a good teammate.

This is a project made by players for players. If you are new to MOBAs and would like some direction with gameplay mechanics, MOBA basics and strategies to help you succeed in Paragon, this is the place for you.

How It Works

The academy is comprised of several players that have a solid understanding of Paragon and MOBAs in general. They have a passion for helping new players, and are excited to help you get started.

Sessions are held once a week and each coach will have his/her own group of players to play PvP, Coop, or custom macthes. Groups are broken down by skill level. Each player will need to play one game with a coach present for evaluation then placement. These sesssions are meant to be fun, informative and above all encouraging and toxic free.

What We Teach

Paragon is a MOBA and we want new players to really understand the genre. Towers, minions, buffs, Orb Prime, wards, lane rotation and much more is what is on the agenda here. Once players understand the basics then we will dive into how to be successful in the world of Agora. How to build decks, hero roles, and the do's and dont's of Paragon.

We hope to educate new players and to even expand the Paragon community.
